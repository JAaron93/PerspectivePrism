üöÄ Getting Started

1

Configure Backend URL

You'll need to connect this extension to a Perspective Prism backend server. This can be:

  • A self-hosted instance running on your own server
  • A local development server (e.g., http://localhost:8000)
  • A trusted third-party service

Note: The backend URL must use HTTPS for security, except for localhost addresses.

2

Test Your Connection

After entering your backend URL, use the "Test Connection" button to verify that the extension can communicate with your server.

3

Start Analyzing Videos

Navigate to any YouTube video and click the "Analyze with Perspective Prism" button that appears near the video player. The extension will extract claims and provide detailed analysis.

üîí Your Privacy Matters

Perspective Prism is designed with privacy in mind:

  • Minimal Data: Only YouTube video IDs are sent to the backend for analysis
  • No Tracking: We don't collect browsing history, personal information, or user identifiers
  • Local Storage: Analysis results are cached locally in your browser
  • Your Control: You can clear cached data and revoke consent at any time

Before your first analysis, you'll be asked to review and accept our privacy policy.
