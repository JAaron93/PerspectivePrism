Quick Visual Checklist

When testing on YouTube, verify:

  • All text is clearly readable (no white text on white background)
  • Button stands out from YouTube's interface
  • Panel has visible borders and shadow
  • Colors match the samples above
  • No dark theme colors are visible
  • Badges have appropriate background colors
  • Confidence bars are blue (#065fd4)
  • Interactive elements have visible hover states
