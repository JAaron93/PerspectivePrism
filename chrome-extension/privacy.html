Privacy Policy

Version 1.0.0 • Effective Date: November 26, 2025

Perspective Prism ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Data Collection

We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our video analysis service:

  • Video ID: When you explicitly request an analysis, we transmit the YouTube Video ID to our backend server.
  • No Browsing History: We do NOT track your browsing history, search queries, or activity on other websites.
  • No Personal Information: We do NOT collect your name, email address, IP address, or YouTube account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

The Video ID is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To retrieve the video transcript and metadata from YouTube.
  • To perform claim extraction and perspective analysis using our AI models.
  • To cache the analysis results locally in your browser for faster future access.

3. Data Retention

  • Local Storage: Analysis results are cached locally in your browser via chrome.storage.local for 24 hours to improve performance. You can clear this cache at any time in the extension settings.
  • Backend Data Retention Policy:
    • Logging: The backend server may temporarily log requests (including Video IDs) for debugging, performance monitoring, and analytics purposes.
    • Retention Period: The retention period for these logs varies depending on the backend implementation. If you are using a third-party backend, their specific retention policies apply.
    • Recommendation: For maximum privacy, we recommend configuring your self-hosted backend to minimize or disable data retention.
    • Third-Party Backends: If you choose to use a third-party backend, you are responsible for reviewing and accepting their data retention policies.

5. User Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your data. However, please note that the Extension itself cannot directly delete data stored on the backend server.

  • Self-Hosted Backend: If you host your own backend, you have full control over your data and can delete logs or database entries directly.
  • Third-Party Backend: If you use a third-party backend, you must contact the backend administrator to request data deletion.

Data Deletion Request Template

You can use the following template to request data deletion from a third-party backend administrator:

Subject: Data Deletion Request - Perspective Prism To whom it may concern, I am a user of the Perspective Prism Chrome Extension and have used your backend service for video analysis. In accordance with applicable data privacy laws, I hereby request the deletion of all data associated with my usage, including but not limited to: - Server access logs containing my IP address. - Any retained Video IDs or analysis results linked to my requests. Please confirm when this action has been completed. Thank you.

6. Your Consent

By using the Extension, you consent to the processing of Video IDs as described in this policy. You can revoke your consent at any time in the extension settings, which will prevent further analysis and clear all locally cached data.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support tab.
